## The advisory shape

You `raise` questions and decisions; the analyst works them at `analyzing` and
parks a recommendation at `recommended` for you — options, tradeoffs, and one
recommendation with its reasoning. You `accept` (a decision, not an execution),
`dig_deeper`, or `defer`. If the analyst is missing a fact only you have, the
task parks at `needs_you` with concrete questions; `answer` sends it back.

**This machine has zero outward edges by design.** The analyst never sends,
books, pays, posts, or moves real files/accounts — it drafts and recommends;
you execute. Keep anything too personal for an agent to process off the board
entirely.
